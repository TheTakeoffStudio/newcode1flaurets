About Little Florets

A School Built on Vision,
Values & Purpose

Little Florets School is a brain child of Sree Sadguru Sai Educational Society, recognized by the Government of Telangana. We follow an integrated curriculum aligned with NEP 2020 and NCF 2023 guidelines.

Our vision is to be the school of parents' choice for transforming child education. Our mission is to provide the right education at the right age, ensuring holistic development that nurtures young minds academically, emotionally, and socially.
